Instructions

  • Factories are the main producers of pollution. Environmental-friendly policies could reduce pollution, but keep in mind that some aggressive policies may hurt the economy and the citizens would not be happy. Balancing between environment and economy is the key to this game. You will win the game if you reduce the pollution to be under 10% and the happiness index is more than 90%. You will lose if the pollution is too high, or economy is hurt too much, or the unrenewable resources is depleted.
